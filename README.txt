This is a project made by an partnership between the FIAP college and the BOSCH company.

The challenge was create an hardware-software sistem that could improve more work productivity in warehouses of BOSCH.

The solution also can help employees and the sistems avoid bad storage or mistakes during the process.

Main.html is the interface that registers the product and sends information to the database. Which will give
information to smartglasses and assist the employee.

The main.cpp is the programming of smartglasses that controls the hardware sistem.
